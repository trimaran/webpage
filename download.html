Download

Trimaran A public release of Trimaran is available for download. You may register and download the full source package.

  • Review the list of software requirements before downloading Trimaran.

  • Read the Trimaran manual for installation instructions as well instructions on how to use and develop in Trimaran.

  • For technical assistance and support, you may contact the Trimaran support team.

SUIF add-on
Frontend		 It is now possible to use the SUIF compiler and its rich collection of high level program analysis passes with the Trimaran compiler and its rich collection of machine specific optimizations. You may register and download our SUIF distribution and the SUIF to Trimaran compiler. Our SUIF distrubution features bug fixes and several enhancements to SUIF version 1.3.0.5.
Benchmarks You may also download benchmark packages for Trimaran.
