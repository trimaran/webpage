Support

For all technical question or to reach other researchers that are using Trimaran, write to trimaran-users at trimaran.org. You must be subscribed to the mailing list in order to post new messages. Please visit the Trimaran users information page to subscribe or for more information.

Before posting a new question, please take the time to review archived postings as your question may have already been answered. Older archives are found here.

For general comments and non-technical question, write to support at trimaran.org.
