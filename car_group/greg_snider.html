Greg Snider

Greg Snider received a B.A. in Physics and M.S. in Scientific Instrumentation from the University of California at Santa Barbara, and an M.S.E.E. from Stanford University. He has worked in the fields of medical instrumentation, telephony, image processing, network protocols, operating systems and programmable logic.

Greg designed the Rebel textual intermediate representation for Elcor and implemented the Rebel reader and writer.

Greg has moved on to a new position within HP.
