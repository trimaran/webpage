Contribute

Trimaran is the result of many contributions from students, faculty, and researchers spanning several years of research and development. If you would like to contribute a new module to Trimaran, please contact support at trimaran.org. The following lists the names of Trimaran contributors to date. If you have contributed to Trimaran but your name does not appear below, please contact support at trimaran.org and let us know.
